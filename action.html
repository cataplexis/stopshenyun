Take Action


Every year, hundreds of venues host Shen Yun performances, unaware of its connection to Falun Gong, a group with a history of cult-like behavior, abuse allegations, and harmful propaganda. Many venues believe they’re supporting cultural diversity and the arts when in reality, they’re giving a platform to misinformation, labor exploitation, and exclusionary ideology.

You can make a difference by urging local theaters, universities, and performance spaces not to rent space to Shen Yun. Personalize this email to send to any venue hosting or advertising Shen Yun performances.

Personalize Your Email






Subject: Concerns About Hosting Shen Yun


Dear Sir or Madam:


I’m writing as a concerned community member to respectfully urge your venue to reconsider hosting or promoting Shen Yun Performing Arts.


Though Shen Yun presents itself as a celebration of Chinese classical dance, it is actually run by followers of Falun Gong, a religious movement with well-documented ties to labor abuse, cult-like control, and anti-LGBTQ+, anti-science ideology. Ticket revenue directly supports this group’s global propaganda efforts, including the spread of misinformation via The Epoch Times.


Recent investigations and lawsuits have revealed troubling practices behind the scenes, including allegations of labor trafficking and psychological abuse of young performers, links to a global disinformation network, and promotion of extremist views on race and medicine. More information about Shen Yun as a cult can be found here. Attached is also a flyer with more details.


Many arts-goers and Chinese American community members feel misled and hurt by Shen Yun’s distorted portrayal of Chinese culture. By hosting Shen Yun, your venue may unintentionally be platforming an organization that does not reflect the inclusive, ethical values of your institution.


Please consider standing with truth and cultural integrity by declining to rent space to Shen Yun. Thank you for your time and for your ongoing commitment to ethical arts programming.


Best Regards,

[Your Name]

[City, State]

[Optional: Your school]

Who to email?

Send this email to venues hosting Shen Yun or to local businesses that advertise Shen Yun performances. Not sure where to start? Try searching up Shen Yun’s tour schedule or past shows in your city. Need help finding organizations to email or writing a personalized version?
