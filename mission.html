Our Mission


Our mission is to spread awareness about the truth behind Shen Yun shows and subsequently reduce their reach. While Shen Yun markets itself as a dance show, it is actually a recruitment and propaganda avenue for Falun Gong, a dangerous cult with unethical treatment of its members and a harmful ideology. Shen Yun dancers do not deserve the control and exploitation leveled at them by Shen Yun.

We believe that audiences, communities, and cultural institutions deserve to know the truth about Shen Yun before making an informed decision about whether or not to support them.

We want to educate the public about the hidden links between Shen Yun, Falun Gong, and The Epoch Times and advocate for performing arts venues to refuse rentals to Shen Yun.
